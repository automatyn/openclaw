When you book a consultation or use our services, we collect your name, email address, and business details you share with us. We use cookies on our website to understand how visitors use the site.

Third Party Services

We use the following third party services that may collect data:

AI Agent Data

When we set up an AI agent for your business, the agent processes messages from your customers. This data is handled by the AI model provider (such as Google, Anthropic, or OpenAI) according to their data processing agreements. We configure agents to minimize data retention and maximize privacy.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All communications are encrypted. API keys and credentials are stored securely and never shared.
